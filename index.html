About me

Helping businesses run smarter.

I'm Michael Abat, an Operations Coordinator and Virtual Assistant based in Baguio, Philippines. I have 18+ years of experience keeping complex operations organized, on-schedule, and audit-ready.

My background is in regulated, quality-audited manufacturing environments (ISO 9001 Internal Auditor). Compliance documentation and attention to detail come naturally to me.

Those systems delivered results: 50% shorter production cycle time, yield lifted from 60% to 96%, and zero non-conformances in an ISO 9001 audit.

I bring that same discipline to VA work. I act as a founder's right hand — coordinating projects, chasing outstanding work, and turning messy information into clean, decision-ready systems.

Industries I support: manufacturing, e-commerce, logistics, and construction businesses that are outgrowing their current systems.
